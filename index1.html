About Us

Blackbox is a premium Health and Fitness space in the heart of the city. We provide our members with the best workouts coupled with personal training to achieve their fitness goals. Our memberships are designed for those who love to train hard and crave variety in their workouts.

Our mission is to change the way people live by transforming the fitness industry. Blackbox was created in 2010 out of a need for a revolutionary training environment that focused on our members' health and lifestyle, not just their physical appearance. We want to create a community where people can get healthy together. We pride ourselves on getting you results through carefully crafted programs, expertly designed spaces and equipment and great music!
